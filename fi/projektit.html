FinHPB

A multicenter FinHPB-collaborative has been established to conduct large-scale clinical, radiological and clinicopathological studies concerning HPB cancers. The collaborative includes one or two senior consultant HPB surgeons as the local principal investigators (PIs) from all academic centers conducting HPB surgery in Finland, as well as senior consultant GI -pathologists, bodyradiologists and biostatisticians.

All patients who have undergone liver, biliary tract or pancreatic resection due to hepatocellular cancer, cholangiocarcinoma or pancreatic cancer in Finland during years 2000 - 2019 are identified from the two nationwide registries (The Finnish Cancer Registry and Finnish Patient Registry) and included, called FinHPB cohort. The follow-up will end on 31 December 2021. The study period will be expanded every 5 years to update the cohort for the most recent data.

Registry data will be collected from The Finnish Cancer Registry, The Finnish Patient Registry, Pension Security Center, Statistics Finland and The Social Insurance Institution (Kela)-registry and patient medical records.
