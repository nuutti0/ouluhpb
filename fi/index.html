Oulun Yliopistollisen Sairaalan
HPB -tutkimusryhmä

Oulu HPB performs clinical and translational research combining surgery, clinical pathology, medical imaging and physics, aiming to identify the patients who benefit from invasive procedures.

Meistä

Oulu HPB is a research group based in Oulu, Finland composed of x post-graduate researchers. We aim to use artificial intelligence to recognize and suggest optimal surgical decisions.
