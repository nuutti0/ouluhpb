Oulu University Hospital
HPB Research Group

Oulu HPB conducts clinical and translational research related to HPB diseases. We combine surgical study questions and data with clinical pathology, metagenomics, medical imaging, and medical physics. Our main research arms are improving early diagnostics, and identifying preoperative prognostic factors for HPB cancers. At the baseline of our research is the FinHPB project, which harbors a nationwide HPB cancer patient cohort.

About us

Our group comprises X post-doctoral researchers and 5-10 doctoral students. We collaborate with several study groups in Finland and internationally. Our main partners at the University of Oulu are clinical pathologists, molecular biologists, and engineers at medical imaging and physics.

FinHPB

A multicenter FinHPB-collaborative has been established to conduct large-scale clinical, radiological and clinicopathological studies concerning HPB cancers. The collaborative includes one or two senior consultant HPB surgeons as the local principal investigators (PIs) from all academic centers conducting HPB surgery in Finland, as well as senior consultant GI -pathologists, bodyradiologists and biostatisticians.
